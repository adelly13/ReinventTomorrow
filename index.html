About Us

  • April 2021

    The Beginning

    As part of Archbishop Mitty's Business Club, we began to notice the trend that low-income and minorities commonly held lower rank roles in the business world. When we sat together to brainstorm on ways to address this issue, we decided to start the social initiative, Reinvent Tomorrow. By raising awareness towards this issue and offering the opportunity to learn more about business, we hope to bring about change and make an impact on the next generation of business leaders.

  • May 2021

    Planning Our First Class

    We decided to start with an Introduction to Business class. We held multiple weekly meetings throughout the month to develop the structure and curriculum.

  • June 2021

    Launching Our First Class

    Currently, we are working on launching our Introduction to Business class. We posted flyers on multiple platforms including Nextdoor and Facebook to hopefully gather more students.

Introduction to Business

This is a completely FREE 4-week summer program where many fundamental business concepts and engaging simulations and activities will be introduced to the students. This program will be held once a week over the course of four weeks from 4:00-5:00PM PST on the dates of June 9th, 16th, 23rd and 30th. Each week, the students will be introduced to a different aspect of business. By the end of this course, students will have a good idea of all that business has to offer.


Syllabus:

  • Day 1 (6/9) What is Business?: Covers topics such as entrepreneurship, supply and demand, economics etc.
  • Day 2 (6/16) What is Finance?: Covers topics such as personal finance, stocks, etc.
  • Day 3 (6/23) What is Sales?: Covers topics such as goods and services, revenue, loss, etc.
  • Day 4 (6/30) What is Marketing?: Covers topics such as marketing, advertising, market research, etc.

Details:

  • For Who: Incoming or current 6th-8th graders
  • Where: Zoom!
  • When: 4:00-5:00 PST on 6/9, 6/16, 6/23, and 6/30
  • Cost: FREE
